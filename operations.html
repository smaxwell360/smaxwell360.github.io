Rolling Out AI Automation Across a 29-Plant Enterprise

Mainstay • AI SaaS & Enterprise Operations
AI Implementation Enterprise Rollout Revenue Impact

The Narrative

When GREIF — a global industrial packaging company — signed on to deploy Mainstay's AI chatbot platform across its manufacturing operations, the implementation scope was substantial: 29 plants, diverse workforces, and a high bar for performance. I led the full rollout, managing everything from configuration and stakeholder alignment to go-live support across all sites. The deployment achieved a 74% autonomous resolution rate, meaning nearly three-quarters of employee inquiries were handled entirely by the AI without human escalation. Beyond operational performance, the engagement directly contributed to $1.5M in ARR — demonstrating that thoughtful, well-executed AI implementation delivers measurable business value at scale.
